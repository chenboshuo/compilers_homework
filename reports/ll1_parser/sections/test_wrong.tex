下面测试非LL(1)文法\ref{eq:wrong}

\begin{equation}
  \begin{array}{ll}
  	S &  \to i E t S S' \\
  		&\;\, |\;\,a \\
  	S' &  \to e S \\
  		&\;\, |\;\,\epsilon \\
  	E &  \to b \\
  \end{array}
  \label{eq:wrong}
\end{equation}

非LL(1)文法在将语法添加到语法分析表的时候会报错，
此时抛出异常如下
\begin{lstlisting}
RuntimeError: It isn't a LL(1) grammar,
            new added M[S'][e] = ['\\epsilon']
            conflict with existing M[S'][e] = ['e', 'S']
            parsing table
\end{lstlisting}
